Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You need a valid driver's license, a credit card, and proof of insurance. International renters also need a passport and an international driver's permit.

Can I rent a car without a credit card?

Yes, some locations accept debit cards, but you may need to provide additional identification and meet certain criteria. Please check with the specific rental location.

Are there age restrictions for renting a car?

Most rental companies require renters to be at least 21 years old. Renters under 25 may be subject to a young driver surcharge.

Is there a mileage limit on rental cars?

Most rentals come with unlimited mileage, but some specialty or promotional rentals may have restrictions. Be sure to check your rental agreement.

Can I add an additional driver to my rental agreement?

Yes, additional drivers can be added for a fee. The additional driver must meet the same requirements as the primary driver.
